  • Pharaonic Village
    Price
  • 150 EGP for person
  • Child up to 10 years 120 EGP
    Location
  • The Pharaonic village is located on the west bank of the Nile River on the island of Jacob and its address is 3 Bahr Al-Azam Street, six miles away from the center of Cairo, and it is close to many hotels
    Village dates
  • The village is open from nine in the morning until five in the evening every day, and in the summer months it extends until seven in the evening
    Village trips
  • The journey inside the village takes from an hour to three and a half hours, depending on what you want to see in the village, and the trip is accompanied by a tour guide.
  • You start by riding a water amphitheater that will pass by the gods and kings of the Pharaonic era with a recording explanation of each scene that passes on it, and after that, representative scenes of Pharaonic life and ancient Egyptian crafts.
  • We see a model from the Karnak temple, the farmer's house, and the ancient Egyptian noble's house, and of course there will be a guide explaining to you the various features that you will pass through.
  • The second part of the trip will be a replica of Tutankhamun’s tomb and a replica of its treasures, which were discovered by Howard Carter in the original tomb
    Deal includes
  • Village trip.
  • Tutankhamun cemetery.
  • 3 museums.
